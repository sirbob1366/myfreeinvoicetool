Stationery is still how serious businesses sound serious

Contracts, offer letters, notices, quotations, bank correspondence — the documents that matter still go out on letterhead. A consistent header tells the reader the page is official, identifies you completely, and survives printing, scanning and forwarding. This letterhead maker gives you that in minutes: logo, business name and tagline, full contact details, three header styles, your accent color, and a discreet footer line — as a clean vector PDF in A4 or US Letter.

Three styles, one identity

  • Classic rule — logo and name on the left, contacts on the right, a strong colored rule with a gold hairline beneath. The default for professional services.
  • Color band — a full-width band in your brand color with reversed type. Confident, modern, unmistakably yours.
  • Centered formal — symmetrical, serif, with a gold hairline divider. The register of law firms and institutions.

Blank pad or finished letter — your choice

Leave the body empty and download a blank branded page: print a stack, or use the PDF as the base layer in any editor. Or type the letter right here — the body is set in a readable serif or sans with proper margins, and long letters flow across pages with the header on page one and the footer throughout.

Matches your invoices, naturally

Use the same logo and accent color you use on the invoice generator and every document you send — bills, estimates, letters — carries one consistent identity. All of it free, none of it uploaded: the letterhead is generated in your browser and belongs entirely to you.
