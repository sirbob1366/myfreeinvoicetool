Questions, answered

Is everything really free? How is that sustainable?

Every feature is free, with no caps. The site is supported by clearly-labeled affiliate recommendations for full accounting suites — relevant only if you outgrow invoicing into bookkeeping, payroll and bank feeds. Your data is never the business model: we couldn't sell what we can't see.

Where exactly is my data stored?

In IndexedDB — your browser's built-in database — on your device. The Workspace reads it locally; the Backup tab exports it as one JSON file you control. Clearing your browser data deletes it, so keep backups of anything important.

Does it work offline, honestly?

Yes. After your first visit, a service worker keeps every page and the PDF engine cached on your device. We test invoice generation with the network disabled — it works. Install it from the address bar (or the “Install app” button) and it behaves like a native app.

Can I move my data to another computer?

Yes — export the single-file backup from the Workspace, open the site on the other machine, and import it. Everything arrives: documents, clients, items, snippets, businesses and numbering sequences.

Is it suitable for GST invoicing in India?

Yes — GSTIN fields for both parties, automatic CGST/SGST vs IGST split from the place of supply, HSN/SAC columns, ₹ formatting with lakh/crore words, April–March financial-year numbering, and a UPI payment QR printed on the PDF.

What's the catch with “no watermark”?

There isn't one. The PDF contains your branding and nothing else — no footer credit, no “made with”, no upgrade nag. We'd rather you tell a friend than be forced to advertise us to your clients.
